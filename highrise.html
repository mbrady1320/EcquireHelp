Highrise

How To Connect To Highrise

  • In your CRM Services screen, select Highrise by clicking the checkbox next to it.
  • Enter your log in information and click "Yes, I'll allow access."
  • Select the company you will be importing contacts to
  • And that's it! You're connected!

Adding A LinkedIn Profile

  • View your contact's LinkedIn profile page.
  • Click the blue Ecquire button
  • Review and add any extra details that you would like to record
  • Click the “Submit to Highrise” button to send your Facebook contact’s information directly to Highrise.

Adding A Facebook Contact

  • Visit your Facebook contact’s profile.
  • Click on “About” at the top of their profile.
  • Click the blue Ecquire button.
  • Review and add any extra details that you would like to record.
  • Click the “Submit to Highrise” button to send your Facebook contact’s information directly to Highrise.

Adding A Twitter Follower

  • Visit a Twitter profile.
  • Click the Ecquire button when it turns blue.
  • Ecquire will pre-fill the contact information. Confirm and add any extra details.
  • Click "Submit to Highrise" to add your Twitter contact to Highrise.

Adding A Contact From Hootsuite

  • Select your contact from the contacts page of your Hootsuite.
  • Click the blue Ecquire button.
  • Ecquire will pre-fill the contact information. Confirm and add any extra details.
  • Click "Submit to Highrise."

Adding A Contact From Angellist

  • While viewing your contact's page, click the blue Ecquire button.
  • Ecquire will pre-fill the contact information. Confirm and add any extra details.
  • Click "Submit to Highrise."

Adding A Contact From Crunchbase

  • While viewing your contact's page, click the blue Ecquire button.
  • Ecquire will pre-fill the contact information. Confirm and add any extra details.
  • Click "Submit to Highrise."

Adding Google Contacts To Highrise

  • View your contacts outside of the Contact Preview mode.
  • Click on the contact you would like to import, then click on the blue Ecquire button.
  • Ecquire will pre-fill the contact information. Confirm and add any extra details.
  • Click "Submit to Highrise."

How To Create Tags

Segmenting Highrise contacts by tags is a great way to quickly understand the context of your business relationships. After you have established a tagging system for your Highrise, adding tags to contacts is easy. Here’s how:

  • Capture a contact from wherever you are working. Whether you’re in Gmail, Linkedin, Facebook, Twitter, HootSuite, or even Google Contacts, Ecquire will be there to add your contact.
  • Click on the “tag” field and enter the tag for your contact.
  • Click the “Submit to Highrise” button to send the contact with the appropriate tag to Highrise."
