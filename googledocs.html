Google Docs

How To Connect To Google Docs

Click on the Settings Icon then click on CRM Services.

Select Google Docs from the available CRMs.

Select the Google account you want Ecquire to connect with. If you have an existing Google Account not listed on the popup window, click "Add Account."

Creating A New Spreadsheet

Once you are connected, Ecquire will prompt you to either select an existing spreadsheet or create a new one.

To create a new spreadsheet, type in the name of your spreadsheet in the text field then click Submit.

By default, Ecquire generates these columns when you create a new spreadsheet:

First Name, Last Name, Email,Company
Phone, Address, Role, Notes
Recent Subject - from Gmail or Inmail,
Recent Body - from Gmail or Inmail,
Source, Date, LinkedIn URL

Connecting to an existing spreadsheet

Choose the spreadsheet from the list to connect to.

Ecquire will detect your spreadsheet's column headers (row 1) and render it in the Ecquire drawer as text fields.


Customizing text fields

Ecquire detects the text fields for the drawer based on what is on the first row of your spreadsheet. Customizing your text fields is as easy as changing the column headers on your spreadsheet.

LinkedIn

  • Ecquire turns blue when it detects data it can capture.
  • The Ecquire drawer pops open when you click the blue icon. It will show you the data it detects for capture. Click your contact's information.
  • Ecquire will ask if you want to create this profile into Google Docs.
  • You can edit the information, choose whether to import as a lead, contact or contact.
  • Click Create in Google Docs when you are ready to import.

Facebook

It’s easy to add a contact to Salesforce directly from Facebook. Here's How:

  • Visit your Facebook contact’s profile.
  • Click on “About.”
  • Click the Ecquire button.
  • Review and add any extra details that you would like to record.
  • Click the “Submit to Google Docs” button.

Twitter

Need to add a contact from Twitter? Here's How:

  • Visit your contact's Twitter profile.
  • Click the blue Ecquire button.
  • Review the information in the Ecquire drawer add required fields and make any changes you would like. (Ensure all required fields are included).
  • Click the “Submit to Google Docs.”

Gmail to Google Docs

When you import a contact from Gmail it will also import your most recent email into Google Docs.

  • View the email from your contact.
  • Click the blue Ecquire button.
  • Review the information in the Ecquire drawer add required fields and make any changes you would like. (Ensure all required fields are included).
  • Click the “Submit to Google Docs.”

You could also update your contact with the most recent email

  • View the email to or from your contact.
  • Click the blue Ecquire button.
  • Review the information in the Ecquire drawer.
  • Click the “Submit to Google Docs.”

InMail to Google Docs

You can import InMail messages to Google Docs.

  • View the conversation with your contact.
  • Click the blue Ecquire button.
  • Choose which message you would like saved.
  • Review the information in the Ecquire drawer.
  • Click the “Submit to Google Docs.”

Search and update an existing contact

  • Enter the first few letters of the contact's name in the searchbar.
  • Select your contact.
  • Remove or add information in the appropriate text fields. Remember, blue means new!
  • Click the “Submit to Google Docs.”
