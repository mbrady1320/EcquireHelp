Pipedrive

How To Connect To Pipedrive

In the Ecquire drawer, click the Settings icon, then click CRM Services.

Select Pipedrive by checking the box to the right. A window will pop open asking you to log in to Pipedrive. Once logged in, you are officially connected!

LinkedIn

  • Ecquire turns blue when it detects data it can capture.
  • Click the Ecquire button. It will show you the data it detects for capture. Click your contact's information.
  • Click "Create This Record In Pipedrive."
  • You can edit the information and choose which stage your contact is in the pipeline. If you have multiple pipelines, you can select which pipeline to add your contact to.
  • Click Create in Pipedrive when you are ready to import. Pipedrive will create your contact as a Deal and also create the Organization your contact is linked to.

Search and update an existing contact

  • In the Ecquire drawer, enter your contact's name in the search bar.
  • Review and make changes to your contact's information. New information will turn blue.
